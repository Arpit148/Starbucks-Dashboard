# Starbucks-Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Starbucks Nutrition & Beverage Intelligence Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze the nutritional composition of Starbucks beverages. By examining calories, sugar, caffeine, and beverage categories, the dashboard provides valuable insights that support healthier menu planning and data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Business Problem

As customers become more health-conscious, Starbucks faces increasing demand for nutritional transparency. With a wide variety of beverages available, identifying products with high calorie, sugar, or caffeine content can be challenging.

This dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

✅ Which beverages contain the most calories?

✅ Which categories contribute the highest sugar intake?

✅ Which drinks have the highest caffeine levels?

✅ How can Starbucks promote healthier beverage choices?

🚀 Project Objectives
📊 Analyze calories, sugar, and caffeine across beverages.
🥤 Compare nutritional values between beverage categories.
⚡ Identify highly caffeinated beverages.
🍃 Support healthier menu recommendations.
📈 Enable data-driven menu optimization.

🔍 Key Insights
⚡ High Caffeine Categories

Coffee-based beverages show the highest average caffeine levels, making them popular among customers seeking an energy boost.

🍬 Sugar Analysis

Several beverage categories contain significantly higher sugar levels, highlighting opportunities for healthier alternatives.

🔥 Calorie Trends

Calorie content varies considerably across beverage categories, helping identify products that may require nutritional optimization.

📊 Product Diversity

The beverage portfolio is heavily concentrated in certain categories, offering opportunities for menu expansion and innovation.
